What we do here

Three threads, woven together. Every event we run pulls from at least one of them. Read more →

01 — NATURE

Walks & hikes

Trails explored, hills climbed, forests rambled — group hikes that build connection and open up space for conversations that don't happen anywhere else.

02 — FITNESS

Outdoor activity

Football, high-rise activities, GoApe and more — movement that builds healthier lifestyles without ever feeling like a gym session.

03 — COMMUNITY

Giving back to the city

Litter picks, charity car washes and other hands-on projects that leave Sheffield a little better than we found it — while building the same sense of connection as our walks and hikes.

Who we are

A Sheffield-based community dedicated to improving mental health by reconnecting people with the outdoors.

Our mission is to support individuals across Sheffield — and beyond — by offering nature-based activities that promote wellbeing, reduce stress and foster a sense of connection.

Together, we're creating healthier minds and stronger communities — one step closer to nature.
