What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They can be used to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, or track your activity across sites for analytics or advertising purposes.

Do we use cookies?

This website does not currently use cookies, analytics tools, or any tracking technology. We don't set cookies to track your visit, remember your preferences, or serve advertising.

Our contact form

The "Join Us" contact form on this website is submitted directly to our form-handling provider, Formspree, without using cookies to do so. Choosing to accept or decline cookies on this site has no effect on whether the contact form works — it will function normally either way, since it doesn't rely on cookies at all.

If this changes in future

If we add analytics, social media embeds, or any other tool that sets cookies in the future, we will update this policy and add a cookie consent banner that lets you choose which types of cookies to accept before any non-essential cookie is set.

Third-party links

This website contains links to third-party platforms such as Instagram and TikTok. If you click through to those platforms, their own cookie policies will apply — we have no control over cookies set by third-party websites.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this cookie policy, please contact us at innercityhealers@outlook.com.
