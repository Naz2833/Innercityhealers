Privacy policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Last updated: 15/07/2026

Who we are

Inner City Healers CIC ("we," "us," "our") is a Sheffield-based community interest company. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any personal information you share with us through this website.

Company registration number: [16245538]
Registered office address: [109 Rock Street, Sheffield, S3 9JB]
Contact email: innercityhealers@outlook.com

What information we collect

When you use the "Join Us" contact form on this website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Any message or information you choose to include

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any other tracking technology on this website at this time.

How we use your information

We use the information you submit through the contact form solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Let you know about upcoming events and activities, if you've asked us to

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How your information is processed

Our contact form is handled by a third-party service, Formspree, which securely receives your submission and forwards it to our email inbox. Formspree acts as a data processor on our behalf. You can read Formspree's own privacy policy on their website.

How long we keep your information

We keep contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or maintain a record of community engagement, and no longer than 12 months.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Object to how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at innercityhealers@outlook.com.

Photos and testimonials

Photos and testimonials featured on this website are used with the consent of the individuals shown or quoted. If you appear in a photo or testimonial on this site and would like it removed, please contact us at the email address above and we will action this promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at innercityhealers@outlook.com.
